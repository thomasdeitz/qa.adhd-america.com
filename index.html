ADHD AMERICA

ADHD America, Inc., a non-for profit organization, provides innovative technology and resources for those children and young adults that are in need of improving their performance in school and in life.

ADHD America, Inc. aims to impact academic excellence through web-based, mobile, and wearable computing applications at NO COST to the ADHD America user by simply joining the "ADHD America Community."
